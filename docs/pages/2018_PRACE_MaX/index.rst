PRACE-MaX tutorial on high-throughput computations
==================================================

The `PRACE-MaX Tutorial on high-throughput computations: general methods
and applications using AiiDA <https://events.prace-ri.eu/event/709/>`__
was held from May 30th to June 1st, 2018 at the Cineca HPC facility in
Bologna, Italy.

The instructions of the tutorial are found below, converted from the
`original LaTeX
source <https://github.com/aiidateam/aiida-tutorials/tree/master/latex>`__
to MarkDown.
